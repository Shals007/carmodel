About us

Tesla is an American electric vehicle (EV) and clean energy company founded by Elon Musk in 2003. The company is known for its groundbreaking electric cars, including the Model S, Model 3, Model X, and Model Y. Tesla has been at the forefront of the EV revolution and is considered a pioneer in the industry. Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy. In addition to its electric cars, the company produces solar energy products, such as solar panels and energy storage solutions like the Powerwall and Powerpack. These products aim to reduce our dependence on fossil fuels and promote sustainable energy practices. Tesla is also known for its Autopilot feature, a driver-assist system that offers semi-autonomous driving capabilities.
